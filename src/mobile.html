Skills Field

Motion Design

I'm deeply interested on all motion graphic technique.
I can offer to animate interactive experiences or UI by programmed, timeline based, both mixed and/or synchronized 2D and 3D motion graphics. Prerender or in realtime.

Front-end development

My web-developer side is focused to give life to what the user manipulate and see. Basically everything related to the technical aspect of the user experience : layout life and adaptation, interactions, programmatic motion.

Compositing

In the goal to synchronize and/or prepare videos for interaction, I’m pretty comfortable with the following compositing techniques: keying, rotoscoping and 2D/3D match moving.
As well, I have a good knowledge of video format and ways to export, integrate videos, track data and drive them by programming.

Research and development

Some projects require pushing the technology into uncharted territory. I can explore this, help to define the technical limits and if agreed upon, integrate the results of experimentation to the main production.

Also, it just so happens that I developed my own tools to simplify the more tedious steps we had to deal with on particular projects.

For instance, on I Kill A Friend and I Kill A Friend 2, [links] I developed a tool to check the position of the 3d mesh in space, the synch movement with the movie, the quality of the rotoscoping, and setup of the 3D lighting.
Likewise, on 208 Greetings [Links], I built an app to record the animation display of the characters which are dynamically generated.

Editing / 3D Modeling

I used to be in charge of the almost entire production process on some projects. It's why I had the occasion to work on video editing and 3D modeling.

For sure this fields are not my speciality, but they cross or approach my main skills such as motion design.
It also give me a pretty good ability in collaborating with video editors and 3D artists / modelers. I can easily fit in a team which is pretty well film-production-oriented.
